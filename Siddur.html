The Shema

The Shema is our acceptance of and submission to the absolute Sovereignty of God.

Immediately before the recitation of the Shema, concentrate on fulfillinng the positive commandment of reciting the Shema daily, once in the evening and once in the morning. Enunciate each word clearly and do not run the words together.
